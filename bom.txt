BOM (Browser Object Model)
--------------------------
Its related to browser's Window :-
Chrome
Internet Explore
Opera 
FireFox
Edge

What we can do ??
--------------------------
Get the width & height of browser Window
open & close the browser Window
move & resize the browser Window
scroll the browser Window
get the url , hostname , protocol of browser Window
history of browser Window

---------------------------------------------------------
Window Height & Width methods:-
---------------------------------------------------------
innerHeight
innerWidth
outerHeight
outerWidth

window.innerHeight
window.innerWidth
window.outerHeight
window.outerWidth

----------------------------------------------------------
Open & Close window methods :-
----------------------------------------------------------
window.open(url , name , specs)
window.close()

----------------------------------------------------------

----------------------------------------------------------

----------------------------------------------------------

----------------------------------------------------------
